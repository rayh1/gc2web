# ADR-0002: Homepage structure centers on purpose and two exploration paths

Date: 2026-06-25
Status: Accepted

## Context

The current homepage is minimal and does not clearly explain the archive or provide strong onward actions. The PRD requires the homepage to communicate the site's purpose and expose at least two primary exploration paths.

## Decision

Restructure the homepage into a purposeful landing page with a hero that states the archive's purpose, explicit primary actions for search and browse, and family-oriented starting sections.

## Consequences

- The homepage gains clearer orientation and stronger entry points without changing the site's route structure.

* Homepage content becomes more curated and sectioned, so decorative additions that do not improve discovery are explicitly excluded.
